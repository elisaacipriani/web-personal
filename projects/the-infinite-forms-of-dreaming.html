The Infinite Forms of Dreaming

Serie
Dream
Material
Textile

This project was an exploration of different types of graphic compositions. Through shapes, transparencies, colors, and textures, a universe was created that represents the infinite ways of dreaming, similar to the imagination of a child at play.

As this project was recognized as a standout piece, she was given the opportunity at Universidad Torcuato Di Tella to develop one of her works further and translate it into the world of textiles.

Selected piece

This piece was later brought to the textile studio El Espartano, where a custom rug was developed from her graphic piece.

Creation of the selected piece transformed into a rug.
